Help with the 6thSense Capture app and EGO cameras

Contact us

For any question, bug report, or help getting set up, email matt@6thsense.dev. We typically respond within one business day.

Quick start

  • Power the camera — 5V/2A supply; wait ~1 minute for the LED to blink. Insert an SD card: all captures are saved to the SD card only.
  • Join its WiFi — connect your phone to the camera's network EGO-XXXXXX (password egocam123). The "no internet" warning is normal.
  • Find & record — open the app's Fleet tab, tap Scan, select your camera, allow Local Network access, and tap the record button.
  • Fleet mode — in a camera's settings → Manage Network, save your site WiFi. Every camera then auto-joins it at power-on, and Record All controls the whole fleet.

Common issues

  • Camera not found when scanning — make sure your phone is on the camera's EGO-XXXXXX network (or the same site WiFi as the cameras), and that Local Network permission is enabled for the app in iOS Settings.
  • Recording won't start — the app blocks recording when no SD card is inserted or free space is below the storage floor (Settings → SD Storage Guard).
  • Starting fresh — Settings → Danger Zone → Reset App restores the app to a fresh-install state, including the setup instructions.
